Type of Bike Insurance

1.Third-Party Liability-Only Cover
Under this policy, if the insured bike is responsible for causing an accident, then any damage to the third-party vehicle and injury or loss of life of the third party shall be reimbursed.


2.Standalone Own Damage Cover
This policy does not cover any third-party liabilities. As a result, this policy can only be bought together with the legally mandatory third-party liability cover.


Is it compulsory to have bike insurance?

Having third-party insurance is anyway compulsory for all vehicles as per the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988

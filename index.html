Why to Buy a Policy?

People typically purchase insurance policies to protect themselves financially from
unexpected events that could cause financial loss.

By paying a relatively small premium, the policyholder transfers the financial risk of
a potential loss to the insurance company.

Ultimately, an insurance policy provides peace of mind by reducing the financial
uncertainty and risk associated with unexpected events.
